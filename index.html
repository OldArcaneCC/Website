Arcane - The Return
Sulium posted 1 week, 3 days ago

On April 11th, we made the decision to shut down VeltPvP This choice was very difficult but after much thought we did not want to compromise our own integrity nor the value and gaming experience that our players have grown accustomed to expect by keeping the server live while our vision could not yet be realized. However, as you'll soon find out, we now have the resources to bring back Velt to its former glory.

Therefore, it is with much anticipation and excitement we are pleased to announce the return of VeltPvP. Through our new angel investor, GodComplex, we’re able to ensure a well maintained and long lasting Velt. We plan to provide a continuing high-quality experience with fun and enhanced game modes for our community.

With the relaunch of our website we want to first announce the return of our Practice and KitMap. They will return very soon in the same state that you remember them. Additionally, we've got a massive amount of updates in the works for the network.

We’re excited to explore this new chapter with you as we restore our community and bring Velt to a whole 'nother level. See you soon.
